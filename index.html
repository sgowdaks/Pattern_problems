Bit Manipulation

    To get the right most set bit: (formula) bit = bit & ~(bit - 1)

    XOR of a number by itself cancels out, this logic can be used to find Non Repeating Numbers types of problems where, with 2 or 1 numbers that is not repeating

    If you want to find the count of 1's in given bits, either using even/odd concept with recursion, or and with 1 and right shift, or bin() from python(bad move!)
